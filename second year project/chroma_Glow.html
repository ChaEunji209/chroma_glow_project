How to consider Undertones?

To test for undertones using the vein test, examine the veins on the inside of your wrist in natural light. If your veins appear blue or purple, you likely have cool undertones. If they look green, you have warm undertones. If you see a mix of both, or if it's hard to tell, you likely have neutral undertones.

Fair

Fair skin tone is similar to porcelain or ivory skin. Fair skin often will have peach, yellow, or olive undertones. It burns easily if unprotected from the sun. People with fair skin will often have pale-colored eyes, and hair color can range from light to dark.

Hair color Tips

For fair skin, a range of hair colors can be flattering, with both warm and cool tones working well depending on the specific undertones of the skin. Generally, warm shades like honey blonde, strawberry blonde, and golden brown enhance the warmth of the skin, while cool shades like platinum blonde, ash blonde, and cool-toned browns provide a striking contrast.
